FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FRAME Magazine?

FRAME is a creative media magazine dedicated to visual storytelling. We bring together photographers, designers, and filmmakers who see the world differently — publishing original stories, tutorials, and behind-the-scenes features every month.

How can I submit my work to FRAME?

We welcome submissions from creators at every level. Visit our Community page to learn about our editorial process, submission guidelines, and upcoming themed issues. We review every submission personally.

What topics does FRAME cover?

FRAME covers photography, graphic design, branding, filmmaking, and visual culture. From golden hour techniques to brand identity systems — if it's visual and creative, it belongs here.

Is there a free subscription option?

Yes! Our core articles and community features are completely free. Premium subscribers get early access to new stories, exclusive tutorials, downloadable resources, and invitations to creator events.

How do I join the FRAME community?

Click the Subscribe button in the navigation or the Join FRAME button below. You'll get access to our creator community, weekly newsletters, and a growing library of visual storytelling resources.
